## About the Smart Internet Cafe

This project was launched by Smart Telecommunications alongside Storage Providers Inc. in 2006, aimed to provide internet access to various provinces that are in vast need of internet connectivity. 
This 40-foot container van consists of 15 computers for rent which makes connecting to the internet much faster as the van is equipped with the latest technology for its time which made internet 2x faster.
